The Airport Book, a book authored in 2022 by Abdur Abou Ramadan, Ahmed Ezzaroui, Sami Abo Ramadan and Juan Rosales, is a book containing information and pictures about every single airport in the world (70 942 to be exact). Let your inner airport nerd take control and go check out the book. Website last updated on 23.12.2022 | 🤎 |
